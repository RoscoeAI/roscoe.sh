Visual Board

  • Poster-scale hero with one big scene, not a dashboard grid.
  • Mechanical motion only: wagon wheels, ticker pulses, brass seal drift.
  • Rye headlines for myth and memory, Spectral body copy for trust and legibility.
  • Compliance surfaces lighten and flatten into almost legal-paper calm.
  • Use woodcut lines, stamp boxes, ticket edges, and rail dividers instead of cards.

Proof Tone

Consent area must stay plain

  • Unchecked opt-in box
  • STOP / HELP visible
  • Rates disclosure visible
  • Privacy + Terms linked near submit
  • No selling or sharing opt-in data
